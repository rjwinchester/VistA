 This patch contains the TBI Work Items completed for this release:
 TBI Patch 2*5 Deployment of Increment 6 Work Items includes the following 
 enhancements and fixes: 
 TBI-51139: Registry Database: The TBI Instruments package shall be 
 expanded to include up to 30 additional clinical templates/instruments. 
 TBI-51140: Registry User Interfaces: Polytrauma Tracking Application 
 shall be expanded.
 TBI- 51141: Veterans Health Information Systems and Technology 
 Architecture (VistA) Components: TBI Instruments package shall be 
 enhanced through improved connectivity with Medical Domain Web Services 
 (MDWS) to streamline Consult Resolution, User Identification and 
 co-signer assignment, appearance of Text Integration Utility (TIU) Notes 
 in CPRS. 
 TBI- 51142: Enhancement of TBI Instruments to improve responsiveness and 
 eliminate all data loss scenarios 
 TBI-51159: Enhancements of Polytrauma Reporting to accommodate additional 
 program groupings and fields, and to catalog multiple entries per patient 
  
 Patch Components:
 -----------------
 The following describes each component of the TBI Patch 2*5
  
 TBI-51139: Registry Database: The TBI Instruments package shall be 
 expanded to include up to 30 additional clinical templates/instruments. 
  
 All templates/instruments in the TBI Instruments package shall be 
 accessible to designated end users via extracts of all data elements, and 
 placed in formatted tables in a manner which permits expansion of 
 existing cube-based and aggregate reporting
  
 TBI-51140: Registry User Interfaces: Polytrauma Tracking Application 
 shall be expanded.
  
 Polytrauma Tracking Application shall be expanded to include additional 
 injuries and fields representing clinical outcomes and demographics of 
 interest for additional cohorts treated through Rehabilitation and 
 Prosthetic Services disciplines (i.e. Amputation System of Care, Blind 
 Rehabilitation System of Care, etc).
  
 TBI- 51141: Veterans Health Information Systems and Technology 
 Architecture (VistA) Components.
  
 Veterans Health Information Systems and Technology Architecture (VistA) 
 Components: TBI Instruments package shall be enhanced through improved 
 connectivity with Medical Domain Web Services (MDWS) to streamline 
 Consult Resolution, User Identification and co-signer assignment, 
 appearance of Text Integration Utility (TIU) Notes in CPRS. 
  
 TBI- 51142: Enhancement of TBI Instruments to improve responsiveness and 
 eliminate all data loss scenarios 
  
 TBI-51159: Enhancements of Polytrauma Reporting to accommodate additional 
 program groupings and fields, and to catalog multiple entries per patient
