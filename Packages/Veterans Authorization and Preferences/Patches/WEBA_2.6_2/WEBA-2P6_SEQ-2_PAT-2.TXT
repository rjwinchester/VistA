Subject: VETERAN AUTHORIZATION AND PREFERENCES (VAP) 2.6.2

Category: 
  - Informational

Description:
============

 NOTE: Technical support staff at Austin Information Technology Center 
 (AITC) will install the enhancement release 2.6.2 on the NVP servers 
 (formerly VLER and formerly NHIN) using a pre-approved Request for Change 
 Order and Implementation Plan. This deployment is scheduled to take place 
 on March 31, 2017 from 8:00 PM EST - 11:00 PM EST. No effort is required 
 by local sites.  
  
 Veteran Authorization and Enhancements (VAP) Release 2.6.2 contains 
 enhancements to the VAP application. These changes are Work Items that 
 were recorded and tracked in the Veteran Authorization and Preferences 
 area of Rational Team Concert, the VA-prescribed tool used for tracking 
 requirements and associated test plans. This release contains the Work 
 Items completed during production deployment.
  
 This release consists of the following enhancement:
  
 -       Modification of the VAP system to use two-factor authentication 
         and re-direct users to the Identity Access Management (IAM) 
         Single-Sign-On Integration (SSOI) service for enabling PIV logon. 
  
 User Acceptance Test (UAT) is by the direction of the VAP Business Owner 
 and no Memorandum of Understandings (MOUs) are required. 
  
 Testing Process: Release 2.6.2 was fully tested though the Office of 
 Information and Technology (OIT) Software Quality Assurance. The VAP 
 Business Owner and Stakeholders completed UAT of enhancements. 
  
 Downstream Dependently System Testing: Testing was done with SSOI, 
 interfacing system.
